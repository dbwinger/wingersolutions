# Privacy Policy

## Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how Winger Solutions B.V. handles personal data collected through this website, contact forms, and quote requests.

## Data We Collect
When you fill out the quote/contact form, we collect:
- Name, email, phone (if provided)
- Company name (if provided)
- Project details and any information you choose to share

This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and provide services.

## How We Process Your Data
- **Form Submission**: Your form data is sent to [Formspree](https://formspree.io/), which forwards it to our email inbox securely.
- **Email Communication**: We retain your email and information to respond and follow up on your inquiry.
- **Retention**: We keep inquiry data for a reasonable period (typically 1 year) for business records, then delete it unless you become a client.

## Third Parties
We use Formspree as a service provider to handle form submissions. Formspree's privacy practices are documented on their [Privacy Policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/).

## Your Rights
Under Dutch/EU law (GDPR), you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- Object to processing
- Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@wingersolutions.nl with "Data Request" in the subject.

## Cookies
This website does not use tracking cookies. Any technical cookies (e.g., for site functionality) are not personal data.

## Changes
